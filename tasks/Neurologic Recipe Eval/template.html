In this HIT, you will be shown a recipe and asked to judge one basic aspect of its quality:

  1. Is the recipe fluent enough to understand, and interpretable as a set of instructions from a recipe?

    • No: The recipe is not understandable or does not form a set of instructions.

Please take care to not submit responses that are uninformed by the instructions.
