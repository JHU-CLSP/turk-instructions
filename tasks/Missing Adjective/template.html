Instructions

We are researchers from the University of Pennsylvania trying to teach computers to understand human language. Help us by completing the following tasks!

You will be shown a series of sentences, each of which has a word missing. Try your best to guess what word is missing.

You must guess at least one word for each sentence, but you may provide more than one guess if you would like. 

  • All of the missing words will be adjectives. 
  • If you list multipe words, please separate the words with white space or commas. 
  • If you provide more than one, please put your "best guess" first, followed by your less confidence guesses.
  • Please only guess single words. You should not use multi-word phrases (e.g. do not use phrases like "very good" or "red hot") 
  • Hyphenated words (e.g. "multi-word", "three-time", "well-known") are okay.
  • Adjectives derived from proper nouns (e.g. "American", "Syrian") as well as common adjectives (e.g. "big", "next", "same") are acceptable.
  • You are not required to do any external research to make sure your guesses are factually correct. E.g. if the sentence mentions a politician with whom you are not familiar, feel free to make assumptions about their nationality based on the sentence without consulting external resources. 
  • There is no one correct answer. We want you to use common sense and real world knowledge to make your best guess possible. You will be approved as long as you appear to be doing the task conscientiously. 

Some examples are shown below

Example 1: The proposed budget bill for the ___ year increased 19.6 percent over the bill of the current year, the news agency reported.

    Good guesses: coming, upcoming, next, new...

Example 2: Mexico's ___ class may not be comfortable with the idea .

    Good guesses: upper, middle, working, ruling...

Example 3: There was no ___ assertion of responsibility .

    Good guesses: immediate, clear, direct...

Example 4: Up to 400,000 of the 1.2 million Poles living in Britain could lose their jobs as the global ___ crisis hits the building and service sectors .

    Good guesses: financial, economic...

Example 5: A day earlier, the ___ military said the rebels were leaving Bamut for eastern Chechnya .

    Good guesses: Russian, US
