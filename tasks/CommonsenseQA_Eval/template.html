What this task is about:

  • The goal is to write questions and answers that are easy for humans and hard for AI machines!.
  • The questions should be related to the given context, and the answers can be reasoned according to the context and commonsense knowledge
  • This is part of a series of small pilot studies. Our goal is to launch an extremely large-scale task (50k - 100k, if turkers seem to do well on our task).

 

How to make the problem easy for humans:

  • Make sure your question can be answered correctly by most people according to the context and common sense.
  • Do not ask a question that is irrelevant to the context and can be directly answered using common sense.
  • Do not ask a question that requires very specialized knowledge that is not common sense.

How to make the problem hard for AI:

  • Ask a question that requires commonsense reasoning . 
  • Craft the correct and incorrect answers as relevant to the context as possible!
  • Craft the correct answer as non-stereotypical as possible!
  • Craft the incorrect answers as appealing as possible!

WARNING: AI is very good at solving your questions correctly based on subtle stylistic cues!
Here are some common mistakes to avoid:

  1. DO NOT craft a question that can be directly answered with a span from the context without commonsense reasoning.
  2. Many turkers subconsciously write longer and more detailed sentences for the correct answer, which AI can easily detect. DO balance your answers.
  3. People subconsciously use exaggerated or extreme language when writing the incorrect answers, which AI can easily detect. DO NOT use exagerated or extreme wordings (e.g., "completely", "not at all", "never", "as much as possible", "as long as possible", "only") unless you use them equally in the correct answer as well.
  4. Many turkers subconsicouly use negative sentiment or informal language more often when writing the incorrect answers, which AI can easily detect. If you use negative sentiment or informal language for the incorrect answers, try to use the same amount for the correct answer as well.
  5. AI can easy solve your problem if your incorrect answer consists of words that are obviously IRRELEVANT with the context. Therefore, try to incorporate words from context as more as possible, but make them incorrect to the question.
  6. AI can easy solve your problem if your incorrect answer consists of words that are obviously very DISASSOCIATED with the correct answer. Therefore, try to incorporate words that are ASSOCIATED with the correct answer as much as possible, but make them incorrect by adding extra details.
  7. AI is also very good at memoryzing specific language patterns associated with incorrect answers, so please vary your language for each HIT and be less predictable or repetitive.
  8. DO try to keep both the length and the style as similar as possible between the correct and the incorrect answer.
