We had two AI systems guess as to what happened between two observed events.

For this task,

  • Read the the observation pair.
  • Then, decide which of the two AI guess is more plausible.

A guess is plausible when:

  • AI's guess is a sensical and coherent follow-up event to Observation 1.
  • AI's guess is a sensical, coherent, and explanatory preceding event to Observation 2.
  • It leaves no large unexplained information gaps.

IMPORTANT Please be forgiving of spelling errors or minor grammatical mistakes. That's not what's being tested here.

Given observation pairs:

Observation 1 Amy got a flat tire on the way home from work.
Observation 2 Amy drove away happy.

Which guess is more plausible?

Example 1
Guess #1
A passerby stopped and helped her change tires.
Guess #2
Amy turned around and gave him the evil eye to stop.
Response Reason for Response
Middle #1 explains what happened to Amy's flat tire that allowed her to drive way. Middle #2 doesn't make any sense given the context.
Example 2
Guess #1
Amy called a road-side assistance.
Guess #2
Amy called road-side assistance who promptly came to change Amy's tire.
Response Reason for Response
Middle #1 only specifies Amy called the mechanics, but it doesn't say she got any help. Middle #2 does specify that Amy got the help she needed for her to drive away happy.

Response Reason for Response
Both sentences are perfectly sensical and coherent explanations given the observations.
